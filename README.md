# 🚀 Blinkit Sales & Delivery Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Blinkit Sales Dashboard is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze and visualize sales performance, delivery efficiency, revenue trends, and key business metrics.

The dashboard processes over 50,000 order records and transforms raw data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, identify revenue-driving categories, and optimize operational workflows.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze sales and revenue performance across different product categories.
- Monitor delivery efficiency and fulfillment metrics.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Track customer ordering patterns and business growth trends.
- Detect operational bottlenecks impacting delivery performance.
- Support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Delivery Success Rate
- Average Delivery Time
- Category-wise Revenue
- Monthly Sales Growth
- Top-Selling Products
- Revenue Contribution by Category

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Revenue Analysis
- Analyzed 50,000+ order records to identify revenue trends and sales performance.
- Discovered that the top-performing product categories contributed approximately 65% of total revenue.

### Delivery Performance
- Evaluated delivery timelines and operational efficiency metrics.
- Identified bottlenecks affecting delivery performance and customer satisfaction.

### Category Performance
- Compared category-wise revenue contributions to determine high-performing business segments.
- Highlighted opportunities for targeted marketing and inventory planning.

### Trend Analysis
- Visualized monthly sales trends and seasonal demand patterns.
- Enabled better forecasting and strategic business planning.
